📊 Global Trade & Economy Guide

The world economy runs on trade. Purchase local exports cheap, and deliver them to far-off kingdoms that import them to maximize dynamic distance-based arbitrage profits!

Kingdom Capital Local Export (Buy Cheap) Import Demand (Sell High)

Core Economic Principles

  • Buy Local Exports: Shipped directly from the source, exports sell at 70% of base price. Buy low!
  • Sell High Imports: Kingdoms that import a good pay a premium of 120% of base price + distance bonus. Sell high!
  • Distance Arbitrage Bonus: Earn a travel reward of +1.5 gold per zone tile of distance between the producing capital and the buying capital.
  • Faction Standings: High faction reputation gives up to 25% discount on buying, and 25% extra payout on selling. Negative standing incurs price penalties.

🧠 trained Merchant AI Arbitrage

When active, the Merchant Trader preset automatically scans the entire world map for cargo price discrepancies. It loads up on local exports, calculates the highest potential payout among all discovered capitals (incorporating dynamic distance bonuses and current faction standing), and automatically updates the player's Target Zone to navigate there.

🛡️ Elden Soul Guide

Controls, Combat, Items & Faction Mechanics

Action Keybind Description
Move Left / Right A / D or Left / Right Arrow Run horizontally through the zone
Jump / Double Jump W or Up Arrow or SPACE Leap into the air. Jump again mid-air to double jump
Block / Duck S or Down Arrow Mitigate damage. Wizards trigger a magic ward dome
Attack Combo CLICK or ENTER or . (PERIOD) Strike with your weapon. Chain clicks/taps for multi-hit combos
Super Skill / Super Spell , (COMMA) Unleash your class's major ability (e.g. 4-hit Samurai stun combo, 7-arrow Ranger spread, 3-orb Wizard burst, Priest team heal, Knight shield charge)
Mega Skill / Mega Spell / (FORWARD SLASH) Cast your class's ultimate elemental spell (e.g. Wizard Blizzard, Spellblade Slash, Pyromancer firestorm)
Summon Companion M Summon a temporary guardian or companion based on your political alignment (Good = Angel/Valkyrie, Neutral = Rogue, Evil = Demon) or cast support heals (Wizard Angel summon)
Dash / Dodge SHIFT Perform a quick dodge dash to phase through attacks. (Can also double-tap A or D)
Interact / Chat F Speak to NPCs, open chests, or inspect town objects
Character & Inventory C Toggle character stats sheet, equip gear, and check level
AI Autoplay Manager Z Toggle the automated AI companion manager overlay
Hide Menu / Interface TAB Toggle HUD and settings UI for screenshots or raw play
💥 Combo Combat System Strike repeatedly in quick succession! Chaining attacks increases damage output. The combo resets if you take a hit or wait too long between swings.
🛡️ Blocking & Defensive Stances Holding S or DOWN reduces all incoming physical damage by up to 50%.
  • Wizard Dome: Playing as a Wizard spawns an active magical barrier dome that completely nullifies spell attacks!
  • Samurai Deflect: Samurai blocking grants a temporary speed and dodge boost.
🎯 Backstabs & Critical Hits Attacking an enemy from behind bypasses portion of their defense and deals 1.5x critical damage. Listen for the satisfying crunch sound!

⚖️ Alignment & Morality Shifts Your decisions during encounters change your standing.
  • Heroic (>20): Unlocks holy blessings, Seraphim faction trust, and discounts.
  • Ruthless (<-20): Unlocks lifesteal artifacts and demon pacts.
  • Neutral (-20 to 20): Balanced boosts via specific artifacts like the Scales of Balance.
🏢 Faction Standing Perks Earning reputation with local political factions yields passive gameplay bonuses:
  • Discounted Goods: Buy items at up to 25% lower prices in friendly faction capitals.
  • Recruitment: Faction guards and royal companions will only join your party if you hold sufficient standing.
